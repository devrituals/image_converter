Privacy Policy

We prioritize user privacy and data security. Your data collection, processing, and use are under your control.

Personal Information:

Your personal information is not collected or shared with third parties. Browsing convertimagefree.com is anonymous and safe. We collect impersonal data such as IP address, file types, conversion duration, and success/error flags for internal performance monitoring. This data is not shared with third parties.

Email Addresses:

Using ConvertImageFree does not require disclosing your email. You can use our service without providing an email within the free tier limits. For higher limits, registration may be required. We assure you that your email and personal data will not be sold or leased for personal or commercial purposes.

Disclosure Exceptions:

We only disclose personal information as required by law or court orders. Disclosure can occur to protect our legal rights or if information poses a physical threat.

User Files Handling:

We do not access, inspect, or copy your files. You have control to delete uploaded files at any time. Input files and temporary files are deleted immediately after conversion. Output files are deleted after 24 hours or upon user deletion.

Security:

Security is paramount. Measures are taken to protect your files and personal data. Communication between hosts and conversion servers is secure, protecting data from unauthorized access.

Your Rights:

You can manage your data and delete output files from your profile. Your account can be deleted easily. Cookies, Analytics, and Ads: We use cookies and Google Analytics for user experience and customize ads through Google AdSense.

Links to Third-Party Websites:

Links are usually within our company's network for safety. Be cautious with third-party links and check their privacy policies.
